## 1. Key Insights

  -Drama is the dominant genre across Prime Video content.

  -Movies significantly outnumber TV Shows, indicating higher focus on movie content.

  -Majority of titles fall under 13+ and 16+ ratings, targeting teen and adult audiences.

  -USA and India are the top content-producing countries.

  -Strong growth in content releases after 2000, with a sharp rise in recent years.
